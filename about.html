Welcome to Our Studio

Tors-Bored Studio is a creative space where traditional pottery meets contemporary design. Each piece is handcrafted with passion, bringing together time-honored techniques with modern artistic vision.

Our studio celebrates the beauty of imperfection and the warmth of handmade objects that tell their own unique stories.

The Craft

Working with clay is a meditation in motion. From the spinning wheel to the kiln's fire, every step in our process is carefully considered and lovingly executed.

We specialize in functional pottery that brings joy to everyday moments – vases that hold memories, bowls that gather families, and vessels that transform spaces.

Our Philosophy

We believe that handmade objects carry the spirit of their makers. Each piece reflects not just skill, but intention, care, and the quiet moments of creation.

In our fast-paced world, pottery reminds us to slow down, to appreciate the tactile, and to find beauty in the simple act of making something with our hands.

Visit Us

Our studio is open by appointment for those who wish to see the creative process firsthand or discuss custom pieces.

We also offer pottery classes for those drawn to learn this ancient art form in a supportive, creative environment.

Contact us to schedule a visit or learn more about our workshops and available pieces.
